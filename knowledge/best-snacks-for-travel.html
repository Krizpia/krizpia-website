What makes a good travel snack?

A good travel snack should be easy to carry, not messy, shareable, tasty and reasonably sturdy in packing. Crispy Kerala snacks can work well for trips when packed carefully.

Kuzhalappam for travel

Kuzhalappam is a good travel snack because it is dry, crispy and easy to share. Traditional Kuzhalappam works for mild snack lovers, while Spicy Kuzhalappam gives stronger flavour.

Thatta for travel

Thatta is also suitable for short trips and office breaks. Its savoury taste makes it a good alternative to regular chips or biscuits.

Travel packing tips

Keep snacks away from heavy items that may crush them. Close opened packs properly. Avoid leaving snacks in hot or humid places for long periods.

When to use travel snacks

Krizpia snacks can be used during family trips, office breaks, school snack boxes, bus or train journeys, guest visits and small gifting occasions.
