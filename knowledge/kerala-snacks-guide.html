Why Kerala snacks are special

Kerala snacks are known for rice flour, coconut, sesame, spices and crispy textures. Many snacks are connected to tea time, festivals, travel and family hospitality. The taste is usually simple, familiar and comforting.

Popular Kerala snacks

Common Kerala snacks include Kuzhalappam, Achappam, Murukku, Thatta, banana chips, tapioca chips, mixture and many local variations. Each snack has its own texture, flavour and occasion.

Tea-time culture

In Kerala homes, tea time is more than a drink. It is a small break for family, neighbours, guests and conversation. Crispy snacks like Kuzhalappam and Thatta fit naturally into this culture.
