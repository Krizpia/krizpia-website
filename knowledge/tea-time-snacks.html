Why tea-time snacks matter

Tea time is a daily habit in many homes. A good snack should be easy to serve, crunchy, tasty and suitable for sharing. Kerala snacks are especially strong in this category because they offer both mild and spicy options.

Best snacks with tea

Traditional Kuzhalappam works well for mild snack lovers, Spicy Kuzhalappam suits those who want heat, Sweet Kuzhalappam adds a light sweet option, and Thatta offers a savoury crispy bite.

Serving ideas

Serve snacks in small bowls or plates so they remain crisp. Keep sweet and spicy items separate. For guests, offering a mix of mild, spicy and sweet snacks creates a better tea-time experience.

Shop and family use

Tea-time snacks work well both for family use and shop counters. Customers often buy crispy snacks for evening use, guests, school snacks, office breaks and travel.

Keeping tea snacks fresh

Always close the pack after opening. Avoid storing near steam from the kitchen or near wet areas. Dry storage helps snacks maintain their crisp texture.
