What is Thatta?

Thatta is a crispy savoury Kerala-style snack known for its flat shape, crunchy bite and mild spicy flavour. It is a simple snack that works well with tea, coffee and casual family snacking.

Taste and texture

Good Thatta should have a clear crisp bite. It should be savoury, lightly spicy and easy to share. Krizpia Thatta is positioned as a tea-time snack with traditional Kerala taste and clean packing.

When to serve it

Thatta is suitable for tea time, shop counters, family snack boxes, office snacks and travel. Since it is flat and crispy, it is easy to pack, display and share.

Why Thatta works in shops

Crispy savoury snacks are often quick impulse purchases. Thatta can attract customers looking for crunchy Kerala snacks, especially when packed neatly and displayed with other Krizpia products.

Storage tips

Protect Thatta from air and moisture. Once opened, close the pack properly or transfer to an airtight container.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Thatta spicy?

It has a savoury and mildly spicy taste.

What is it best served with?

Tea and coffee are the best pairings.

Is it suitable for shops?

Yes, it is shop-supply friendly and easy to display.

How should it be stored?

Store in a cool, dry place and keep sealed.
