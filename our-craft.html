Our Craft

Small-batch Kerala snacks made with visible quality controls

Krizpia is an authentic South Indian snack brand manufactured by Rangeela Products in Changanacherry, Kottayam. Our production narrative is simple: controlled batches, hygienic handling, direct quality checks and clear pouch packaging that lets buyers inspect the snack before purchase.

Step 1: Small-Batch Blending

Ingredients such as rice flour, coconut, cumin, fennel and sesame are proportioned and blended in controlled batches. This small-batch approach helps keep taste, aroma and texture consistent across retail packs and larger wholesale supply runs.

Step 2: Hygienic Shaping

Manual rolling and preparation methods are carried out at our Changanacherry facility under FSSAI Licence No. 21326171000212. The process is organized to support traditional shaping while maintaining food-safety discipline for direct consumer orders and retail distribution.

Step 3: Double Safety & Quality Checks

During preparation, the team checks taste and crispness so each batch matches Krizpia’s expected Kerala snack profile. After packing, a final seal inspection helps prevent ambient moisture exposure and protects crunch during storage and delivery.

Step 4: Clear Pouch Packaging

Krizpia uses clear pouch packaging so retail shop owners and consumers can instantly inspect color uniformity and crunch condition before purchase. This visibility supports confidence for shelf stocking, event orders and family snack buying.
