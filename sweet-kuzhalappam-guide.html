Sweet Kuzhalappam Guide

Sweet Kuzhalappam gives a gentler flavour for children, guests and festive snack moments.

When to choose sweet Kuzhalappam

Sweet snacks are useful when serving children, visitors or family gatherings where not everyone wants spice. The crisp tube shape gives the traditional Kuzhalappam experience with a softer taste profile.

Keep the pack sealed after opening so the snack stays crisp and fresh for the next tea-time serving.

Storage and serving tips

Store Krizpia snacks in a cool, dry place away from sunlight, moisture and strong odours. After opening, keep the pack sealed or transfer the snacks to an airtight container to maintain crispness.

Serve with tea, coffee, fresh fruit or other Kerala snacks. For family plates, combine Traditional Kuzhalappam, Spicy Kuzhalappam, Sweet Kuzhalappam and Thatta so guests can choose their preferred taste.
