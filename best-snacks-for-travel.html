Best Kerala Snacks for Travel

Good travel snacks should be dry, easy to share, less messy and able to stay crisp when stored correctly.

What makes a good travel snack?

For bus trips, train journeys, school snacks and family outings, packed Kerala snacks are convenient because they do not need reheating. Kuzhalappam and Thatta are easy to carry, easy to serve and pair well with tea or coffee stops on the way.

Choose sealed packs, keep them away from moisture and close the pack after opening. For group travel, carry a mix of mild, spicy and sweet snacks so everyone has an option.

Storage and serving tips

Store Krizpia snacks in a cool, dry place away from sunlight, moisture and strong odours. After opening, keep the pack sealed or transfer the snacks to an airtight container to maintain crispness.

Serve with tea, coffee, fresh fruit or other Kerala snacks. For family plates, combine Traditional Kuzhalappam, Spicy Kuzhalappam, Sweet Kuzhalappam and Thatta so guests can choose their preferred taste.
