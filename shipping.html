Shipping Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Krizpia is committed to delivering fresh and authentic Kerala snacks to our customers in a safe and timely manner.

1. Order Processing

Orders are normally processed within 1–3 business days after order confirmation and payment verification.

2. Delivery Time

Delivery times may vary depending on the destination, courier service, weather conditions, holidays, and other operational factors.

3. Shipping Areas

Currently, Krizpia products are supplied within India. Availability of shipping to additional locations may be announced in the future.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, will be communicated at the time of order confirmation.

5. Delivery Delays

While we strive to ensure timely delivery, Krizpia shall not be held responsible for delays caused by courier services, natural events, transportation disruptions, or circumstances beyond our control.

6. Damaged Packages

Customers are advised to inspect packages upon delivery. Any visible damage should be reported immediately with supporting photographs.
