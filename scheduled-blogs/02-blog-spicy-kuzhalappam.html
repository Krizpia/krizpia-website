What is Spicy Kuzhalappam?

Spicy Kuzhalappam is a traditional Kerala snack made primarily from premium rice flour and blended with aromatic spices. Unlike the classic version, this spicy variety includes carefully balanced chilli, pepper, garlic, ginger, and traditional Kerala seasonings that deliver a bold yet enjoyable flavour. Every bite offers the perfect combination of crunch and spice, making it one of Kerala's most loved tea-time snacks.
