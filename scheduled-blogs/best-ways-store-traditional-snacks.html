Best Ways to Store Traditional Snacks

Traditional fried snacks stay at their best when protected from air, moisture and direct sunlight. Store sealed packs in a cool, dry place, close opened packs tightly and avoid keeping snacks near strong-smelling foods. Always check the best-before date and the condition of the pack before serving.

Quick serving tip

Serve only the quantity needed, then seal the remaining pack immediately. This keeps the snack crisp for the next tea-time moment.
