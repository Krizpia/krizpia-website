Best Kerala Snacks for Office Tea Breaks

Office tea breaks need snacks that are easy to share, tidy to serve and satisfying with hot tea or coffee. Traditional Kuzhalappam is mild and crunchy, Spicy Kuzhalappam adds a bold bite, Sweet Kuzhalappam suits lighter sweet cravings and Thatta works well for savoury snack plates. Keep packs sealed until serving so the texture stays crisp.

Quick serving tip

Serve only the quantity needed, then seal the remaining pack immediately. This keeps the snack crisp for the next tea-time moment.
