Kerala Tea Time Culture

Kerala tea time is about more than a drink. It is a pause in the day, a welcome for visitors and a time to share crisp snacks. Traditional products such as Kuzhalappam and Thatta fit naturally into this culture because they are easy to serve and enjoy.

Simple serving idea

Open packs close to serving time, use a dry spoon or clean hands, and keep the remaining snacks sealed for better crispness.
