What is Kerala Thatta?

Kerala Thatta is a crispy traditional snack made mainly with rice flour and selected spices. It is known for its flat crispy texture, spicy flavour, and satisfying crunch.

Krizpia Thatta is crafted to deliver the authentic Kerala snack experience in every bite. It is perfect for people who enjoy spicy, crunchy, and flavourful snacks.
