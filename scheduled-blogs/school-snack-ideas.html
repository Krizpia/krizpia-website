School Snack Ideas

School snacks should be easy to pack, easy to share and simple to serve in small portions. Mild Kuzhalappam can be paired with fruit, Sweet Kuzhalappam can be used for occasional treats and Thatta can add savoury crunch. Always pack snacks in a dry compartment.

Simple serving idea

Open packs close to serving time, use a dry spoon or clean hands, and keep the remaining snacks sealed for better crispness.
