Homemade Kerala Snacks

Homemade Kerala snacks are loved because they connect everyday tea time with family tradition. Crisp texture, familiar spices and simple serving make snacks like Kuzhalappam and Thatta easy to enjoy with guests, children and neighbours. Krizpia keeps that home-style memory in convenient packs for modern routines.

Simple serving idea

Open packs close to serving time, use a dry spoon or clean hands, and keep the remaining snacks sealed for better crispness.
