What is Sweet Kuzhalappam?

Sweet Kuzhalappam is a traditional Kerala snack made using rice flour and carefully prepared with a mild sweet flavour. It keeps the classic crispy tube-shaped texture of Kuzhalappam while adding a pleasant sweetness that both children and adults can enjoy.

Unlike heavy sweets, Sweet Kuzhalappam is light, crunchy, and easy to enjoy at any time of the day. It is especially loved as an evening snack with tea or coffee.
