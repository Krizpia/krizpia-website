Authentic Kerala Snacks: What Makes Kerala Crunch So Special

Kerala snacks are known for crisp texture, warm spices and recipes built for sharing. Kuzhalappam and Thatta are everyday favourites because they pair easily with tea and stay convenient for family serving, travel and retail shelves. Krizpia focuses on familiar taste, clear pack information and product pages that help customers choose mild, spicy or sweet options.

Quick serving tip

Serve only the quantity needed, then seal the remaining pack immediately. This keeps the snack crisp for the next tea-time moment.
