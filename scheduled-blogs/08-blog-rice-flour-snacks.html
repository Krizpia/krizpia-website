Why Rice Flour Snacks Are Popular in Kerala

Rice flour is one of the most loved ingredients in Kerala snacks. It gives a light texture, crispy bite, and traditional taste that makes every snack enjoyable.

From Kuzhalappam to Thatta, rice flour snacks are commonly served with tea, shared during festivals, and enjoyed as a crunchy snack at home.

“The best Kerala snacks are simple, crispy, and full of traditional flavour.”
