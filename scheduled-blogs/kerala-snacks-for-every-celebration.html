Kerala Snacks for Every Celebration

Celebrations feel complete when there is something crisp to pass around. Kerala snacks work well for birthdays, festive visits, office sharing and family evenings because they do not need complicated serving. Combine mild, spicy and sweet choices so every guest can pick a favourite.

Simple serving idea

Open packs close to serving time, use a dry spoon or clean hands, and keep the remaining snacks sealed for better crispness.
