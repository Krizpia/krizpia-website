Kerala Snacks Guide – Traditional Crispy Snacks Loved by Every Family

Kerala is famous for its crispy, flavourful, and traditional snacks. This guide helps you explore some of the most loved Kerala snacks from Krizpia.

Why Kerala Snacks Are So Special

Kerala snacks are known for their unique taste, simple ingredients, and traditional preparation style. From crispy rice flour snacks to spicy tea-time favourites, every snack has a special place in Kerala homes.

These snacks are commonly enjoyed with evening tea, served to guests, packed for travel, and shared during festivals and family gatherings.

“Kerala snacks are not just food; they are part of family memories, tea-time conversations, and festive celebrations.”

Popular Kerala Snacks from Krizpia

1. Traditional Kuzhalappam

Traditional Kuzhalappam is one of Kerala’s classic crispy snacks made using rice flour and traditional ingredients. Its tube-like shape, crunchy texture, and authentic flavour make it a favourite snack for all age groups.

2. Spicy Kuzhalappam

Spicy Kuzhalappam is perfect for people who enjoy a little heat in their snacks. It combines the classic crunch of Kuzhalappam with bold Kerala-style spices.

3. Sweet Kuzhalappam

Sweet Kuzhalappam offers a light sweet taste with a crispy bite. It is ideal for children, family sharing, festivals, and tea-time snacking.

4. Kerala Thatta

Kerala Thatta is a flat, crispy, spicy snack made with rice flour and selected spices. It is crunchy, bold, and perfect for evening tea or travel snacking.

How to Choose the Right Kerala Snack

If you love classic flavour, choose Traditional Kuzhalappam. If you prefer spicy snacks, Spicy Kuzhalappam or Thatta will be a great choice. If you like a mild sweet taste, Sweet Kuzhalappam is perfect.
