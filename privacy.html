Welcome to Krizpia, a premium Kerala snack brand owned and operated by Rangeela Products. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us through our website, email, telephone, social media platforms, or enquiry forms.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and manage your personal information when you visit www.krizpia.com or communicate with us.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Krizpia, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Mobile or phone number
  • Delivery address or location, where applicable
  • Product or distributor enquiry details
  • Messages or feedback submitted through our website
  • Optional analytics information, collected only after you choose to turn analytics on, such as browser type, device type, approximate city/country, traffic source, page views, visitor counts, clicks, and enquiry form submission events.
  • Meta Pixel page-view information, which may include pages visited and standard browser or device signals used by Meta to measure advertising performance.

You may choose not to provide certain information; however, this may limit our ability to respond to your enquiry or provide our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected may be used to:

  • Respond to product and customer enquiries.
  • Process orders and business enquiries.
  • Communicate regarding products, deliveries, and customer support.
  • Respond to wholesale or distributor requests.
  • Improve our products, website, and customer experience.
  • Send important updates relating to Krizpia products or services, where permitted by law.
  • Meet legal and regulatory obligations.

We collect only the information necessary for legitimate business purposes.

3. Information Sharing

Krizpia respects your privacy. We do not sell, rent, trade, or disclose your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Information may be shared only:

  • When required by applicable law.
  • With trusted service providers assisting in order fulfilment or delivery.
  • To protect our legal rights or prevent fraud.

Any third-party service providers are expected to handle your information responsibly.

4. Cookies and Website Analytics

Essential cookies are used for site function. Analytics cookies are optional and are not used unless you choose to turn analytics on. Krizpia also uses Meta Pixel to measure page views and advertising performance. If enabled, analytics tools help us measure:

  • Visitor counts and page views.
  • Approximate city and country derived from network information; we do not request precise GPS location.
  • Device category, browser, and general website performance.
  • Traffic source, such as direct visits, search, social media, referral websites, or campaign links.
  • Website interactions, including link clicks, product enquiry actions, and successful form submissions.

Optional analytics is opt-in. It stays off unless you select “Turn on analytics” in the cookie banner. If you select “Keep analytics off,” we do not load optional analytics or send optional click events. Meta Pixel may still collect page-view events to help measure advertising performance. Optional analytics is configured to reduce privacy impact by anonymising IP addresses where supported and disabling advertising personalisation signals. Essential security cookies may still be used to protect forms and prevent misuse.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Although we strive to protect your information, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
