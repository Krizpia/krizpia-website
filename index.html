Our Snack Range

Krizpia brings a wide range of authentic Kerala snacks made using traditional Kerala recipes and quality ingredients. Perfect for tea time, family gatherings, gifting, and retail stores.

Traditional Kuzhalappam

A classic Kerala tea-time snack made with rice flour, coconut, sesame and spices.

  • Best for tea time
  • Crunchy mild flavour
  • Authentic Kerala recipe

Spicy Kuzhalappam

A bold, crispy Kuzhalappam with chilli, pepper, ginger and garlic notes.

  • Rich spicy taste
  • Crispy roll shape
  • Evening snack favourite

Sweet Kuzhalappam

A sweet version of the Kerala favourite with a glossy finish and gentle sweetness.

  • Sweet and crunchy
  • Great for celebrations
  • Kids and guests

Thatta

A thin, round and crispy Kerala-style savoury snack with a light crunch and mild spice.

  • Perfect with tea
  • Easy to share
  • Shop supply friendly

From Raw Materials to Freshly Packed Krizpia

See how our Kerala snacks are prepared with care from start to finish.

🌾

Raw Material Selection

Rice flour, coconut, onion and spices are carefully selected.

🔪

Ingredient Preparation

Fresh ingredients are cleaned, prepared and measured.

🥣

Dough Mixing

Ingredients are mixed into smooth snack dough.

✂️

Cutting

The dough is divided into small portions.

⚙️

Sheeting

Each portion is flattened evenly using a sheeting machine.

🙌

Hand Rolling

The sheets are rolled by hand into traditional shapes.

🔥

Frying

The snacks are fried until golden and crispy.

👀

Quality Check

Every batch is checked for taste, crunch and appearance.

📦

Packing

Fresh snacks are hygienically packed for freshness.
