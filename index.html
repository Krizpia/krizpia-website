Our Snack Range

Krizpia brings a wide range of authentic Kerala snacks, including Traditional Kuzhalappam, Spicy Kuzhalappam, Sweet Kuzhalappam, and Thatta. Made using traditional Kerala recipes and quality ingredients, our snacks are perfect for tea time, family gatherings, gifting, and retail stores.

Traditional Kuzhalappam

A classic Kerala tea-time snack made with rice flour, coconut, sesame and spices. Light, crispy rolls with a homely traditional taste.

  • Best for tea time and family sharing
  • Crunchy texture with mild flavour
  • Inspired by authentic Kerala recipes

Spicy Kuzhalappam

A bold, crispy Kuzhalappam with chilli, pepper, ginger and garlic notes. Perfect for customers who enjoy a stronger snack flavour.

  • Rich spicy colour and taste
  • Crispy roll shape
  • Ideal for evening snacking

Sweet Kuzhalappam

A sweet version of the Kerala favourite with a glossy finish and gentle sweetness. Great for kids, guests and festive snack boxes.

  • Sweet and crunchy finish
  • Great for celebrations
  • Kerala-style handmade taste

Thatta

Thatta is a thin, round and crispy Kerala-style savoury snack prepared with rice flour, dal flour, sesame seeds, spices and curry leaves. It has a light crunch, mild spice and traditional homemade taste.

  • Perfect with tea or coffee
  • Easy to share with family and guests
  • Great for shop supply and local snack counters
