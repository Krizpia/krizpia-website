Quality & Safety Assurance

Batch Quality Checks

Each batch is checked for appearance, taste, crispness, packing condition and freshness before dispatch to shops and customers.

Moisture Protection

Our packaging is selected to help reduce moisture exposure and maintain the crisp texture of Kerala snacks during storage and handling.

Clean Preparation

We follow clean preparation, controlled handling and hygienic packing practices to protect the quality of every product.

Allergen Awareness

Allergen information is clearly shared to help customers make safer food choices before purchase and consumption.

Storage Guidance

For best crispness, store products in a cool, dry place away from sunlight, moisture and strong odours. Keep the pack sealed after opening.
