Complete Kerala Snacks Guide

Kerala snacks are known for coconut, rice flour, sesame, spice blends and tea-time crunch.

Popular Kerala snack styles

Kerala snack culture includes crispy fried snacks, sweet festival bites, spicy tea-time items and sharing packs for guests. Kuzhalappam is loved for its tube shape and crisp texture, while Thatta is a flat savoury option with a satisfying crunch.

Krizpia focuses on packed Kerala snacks that feel traditional but are convenient for modern families, shops and gifting.

Storage and serving tips

Store Krizpia snacks in a cool, dry place away from sunlight, moisture and strong odours. After opening, keep the pack sealed or transfer the snacks to an airtight container to maintain crispness.

Serve with tea, coffee, fresh fruit or other Kerala snacks. For family plates, combine Traditional Kuzhalappam, Spicy Kuzhalappam, Sweet Kuzhalappam and Thatta so guests can choose their preferred taste.
