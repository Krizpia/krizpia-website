Healthy Kerala Snacks Guide

A balanced snack choice depends on portion size, ingredients, freshness and how the snack fits into your routine.

Smarter snacking habits

Kerala snacks are best enjoyed in sensible portions with tea, coffee or family meals. Dry crispy snacks can be easier to store and share than many fresh snacks, but they should still be eaten mindfully.

Check allergen information, store packs in a cool dry place and choose flavours based on your preference. Sweet, mild and spicy options help families serve different tastes without over-ordering.

Storage and serving tips

Store Krizpia snacks in a cool, dry place away from sunlight, moisture and strong odours. After opening, keep the pack sealed or transfer the snacks to an airtight container to maintain crispness.

Serve with tea, coffee, fresh fruit or other Kerala snacks. For family plates, combine Traditional Kuzhalappam, Spicy Kuzhalappam, Sweet Kuzhalappam and Thatta so guests can choose their preferred taste.
