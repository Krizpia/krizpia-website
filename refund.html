Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At Krizpia, we are committed to providing high-quality Kerala snacks. Due to the nature of food products, our refund and return policy is outlined below.

1. Food Products

As food items are perishable products, returns are generally not accepted once the product has been delivered.

2. Damaged or Incorrect Products

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery with clear photographs of the product and packaging.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds or replacements may be considered only in cases where:

  • The product was damaged during transit.
  • An incorrect product was delivered.
  • The product quality is found to be defective upon verification.

4. Refund Process

Once the issue is reviewed and approved, refunds will be processed through the original payment method or an alternative mutually agreed method.

5. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after purchase.
  • Products opened or consumed after delivery.
  • Incorrect storage by the customer.
  • Minor variations in appearance, color, or packaging.
